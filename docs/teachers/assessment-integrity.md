# Assessment integrity

When you create or edit an assignment, you can adjust the **Assessment integrity** options under **More options**. These settings help students stay focused during the activity.

## Copy and paste

- **Off (default):** Students cannot paste into text chat or static text answers.
- **On:** Students can paste as usual.

## Leaving the browser tab

You choose what happens when a student moves away from the assignment (for example, to another tab or window):

- **Allow** — Students are not warned or blocked.
- **Warn** — When they come back, they see a short reminder to stay on the assignment.
- **Block after several leaves** — You set how many times they may leave before their access to the activity is **suspended**. After that, they cannot continue until you restore access.

## When a student's access is suspended

In order to restore access, the student has to contact the teacher for restoring the access.

## Restoring access

1. Open the assignment and go to **Submissions**.
2. Open that student’s submission.
3. If access was suspended for integrity reasons, you will see a notice. Choose **Restore student access**.

After you restore access, the student can continue the activity. Their tab-leave count for that attempt starts fresh so a single mistake does not keep them locked out.
